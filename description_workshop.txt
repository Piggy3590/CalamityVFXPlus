CalamiFX+ is a mod that applies [b][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3628420060&searchtext=vfx%2B]VFX+[/url][/b] style effects to weapons in [b][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2824688072&searchtext=calamity+mod]Calamity Mod[/url][/b].

While some shaders have been modified, the foundation is entirely based on the work of [b]Linty (@linty9075)[/b], who developed [b]VFX+[/b]. Thank you for the awesome mod!

This mod shouldn't affect weapon behavior, but it's not a perfect implementation, so there may be slight differences.

[h3]Tested only on Calamity 2.1.1![/h3]

==============
